# Code of Conduct

## Expected behavior

Participants in this project are expected to:

- be respectful and direct
- assume good intent while reviewing ideas critically
- give actionable technical feedback
- avoid harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks
- keep discussions focused on improving the software

## Unacceptable behavior

This includes:

- abusive or demeaning language
- harassment or intimidation
- discrimination based on personal characteristics
- doxxing or sharing private information without permission
- repeated bad-faith disruption of project discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block contributors who violate this policy.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainer privately with relevant context and links.
